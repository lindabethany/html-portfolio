The Best Movies According to Linda

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Gone with the Wind

A beautiful film that encapsulates civilian sentiments surrounding The Civil War.

Pride and Predjudice

The music, the cinematography, the acting is all chef's kiss. And that hand scene still makes me swoon.

A Walk to Remember

This one is definitely a tear jerker, but the biggest lesson is that people can change and evolve.
